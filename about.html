About

I am an 18-year-old Creative Developer and Entrepreneur based in Mumbai, currently completing my SYJC in Computer Science at Lords Universal College. My journey in technology began with a deep curiosity about how systems communicate—driving me to explore everything from low-level 8085 microprocessor assembly languages to deploying scalable, high-performance cloud architectures.

I believe the best products sit at the intersection of flawless logic and striking aesthetics. That’s why I balance my core software engineering skills with an eye for Graphic Design and Video Editing. Whether I am architecting web hosting infrastructure using cPanel and WHMCS, or designing an intuitive, customer-loved brand identity, I ensure that the user experience feels premium from the first click to the final deployment.

Beyond the screen, I am deeply passionate about hardware and IoT. I have built autonomous robotics and assistive technologies using Arduino and ESP32 microcontrollers, such as a smart, autonomous waste management system and an AI-powered voice assistant. I love bridging the physical and digital worlds to solve genuine, real-world problems.

On the business side, I am the Co-Founder of ZNOir, a sustainable clothing rental startup where I hold a 30% equity stake. We are on a mission to disrupt fast fashion by making high-quality clothing accessible, eco-friendly, and affordable for students and young professionals. I am continuously exploring new frameworks, building the broader Xaltron ecosystem, and seeking out new freelance or collaborative opportunities.
